Download Bitcoin Classic

Classic is capable of following the Bitcoin chain (BTC) or the Bitcoin Cash chain (BCC), you just have to tell it which one you want it to follow.

Users can tell Classic which chain to follow using two options;

  • download plain Classic and configure it using the uahf=[true|false] option to turn on/off UAHF. UAHF is the working name for the Bitcoin Cash chain. So passing in -uahf=true means your client will show BCC in the GUI and follow Bitcoin Cash rules. Specifically, you can run classic like this (may want to replace the dirs with something more sane)
    • bitcoin-qt -datadir=c:\cash -uahf=true
    • bitcoin-qt -datadir=c:\bitcoin -uahf=false -listen=0
  • Or you can use the pre-configured downloads below. The main difference between those two binaries is that the -uahf=true is already pre-configured in the Bitcoin Cash download.
